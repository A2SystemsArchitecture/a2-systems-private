DOK 3 & DOK 4 · Randomized · Graded Assessment

Examination Instructions

You will be assigned a randomly selected character and scenario from Romeo and Juliet. You must engage in a structured Socratic dialogue — responding to questions, citing specific textual evidence, and demonstrating analytical mastery of the relevant Georgia ELA Standards.

📝
Minimum Response
You must write a minimum of 350 words across your dialogue responses before you may submit for a grade.
📖
Textual Evidence
Every analytical claim must be supported by specific references to the play — cite acts, scenes, and lines where possible.
🎭
Stay In Character
You are roleplaying as your assigned character. Your responses should reflect that character's perspective and situation in the play.
⚖️
Grading
Your dialogue will be scored 0–100 based on textual evidence, analytical depth, character accuracy, and thematic understanding.
